Location

The wedding will take place in a lovely restaurant in Žalec called Galerija okusov. You can visit their website here: https://www.galerijaokusov.si/en/home

If the weather is nice, the ceremony and dinner will be held outside, in front of the restaurant, and we will move inside in the evening. However, if it will rain, the ceremony will be in the nearby manor and then the dinner and party inside the restaurant. But no matter what the weather is, you come to the restaurant and we will make sure you will know where to go then :)

There is a parking space in front of the restaurant for everyone. However, there will be a shuttle transport to and from the venue so if you will be staying nearby, you won't have to worry about transport and you will be able to get to the wedding and back without issues.
