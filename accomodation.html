Accomodation

In order to make sure you have a lovely and relaxed time and that don't have to worry about transportation, there will be a shuttle available to get you from your accommodation to the wedding venue and back - within a reasonable radius of course (up to 15km or so).

We suggest you look at the following options and choose one that you like. When you make the reservation, let us know, so that we will be able to tell the shuttle driver and arrange your transportation.
